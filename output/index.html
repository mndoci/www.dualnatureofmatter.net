The Dual Nature of Matter is a musical project from Deepak Singh, usually exploring various abstract sonic landscapes

You can find all my music on Soundcloud.

All music is recorded in a small home studio that has evolved over the years from an almost exclusively software-based setup (with hardware controllers) to a mixed hardware/software setup.
